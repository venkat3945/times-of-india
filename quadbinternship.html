Rohit ruled out of SA Test series with hamstring injury


India's Test vice-captain Rohit Sharma has been ruled out of the entire 3-Test series against South Africa as his old left-hamstring injury resurfaced during the team's net session in Mumbai. The board has not announced a stand-in vice-captain but it is expected that KL Rahul is the front-runner for being Virat Kohli's deputy during the Tests.
